Abilities

Ink Saver Main is the cornerstone ability of almost any E-Liter. The average build sees anywhere from 9â€“30 AP dedicated strictly to this ability. 10 AP is enough for a full extra shot. On top of that, low amounts of Ink Saver Main have a tremendous effect on how long it takes to refill ink for another shot. This is an ability you never want to sleep on.

Ink Saver Sub can be useful at times on Vanilla E-Liter. The weapon as a whole is pretty ink hungry, and sometimes you may find yourself out of ink when attempting to place one in a panic. 6 AP stacks well with 10 AP of Ink Saver Main in personal experience. Overall, this ability is far from necessary, but it can very well come in handy at times.
